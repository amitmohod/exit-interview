Actionable insights
Career growth crisis

34% cite career advancement as #1 reason. Only 29% rate growth potential as good/excellent. Structured career pathing urgently needed across operational roles.

Recognition gap

Only 31% of supervisors "almost always" provide recognition. Exits citing lack of recognition grew 8pp YoY. Manager recognition training is high-ROI.

Ground ops burnout

22% dept attrition — 54% say workload too great. Matches industry benchmark of ~20%. Immediate resourcing review recommended.

Early tenure exits

Avg tenure dropped from 4.1 to 3.2 yrs. 41% of exits happen in years 2–3. Onboarding and early career experience need a structural fix.

Flex work competitive gap

29% say new employer offers flexible work that Etihad doesn't. Corporate functions should review hybrid policy as a retention lever.

Policy compliance is strong

67% rate supervisors "almost always" on policy compliance — highest Q3 dimension. A foundation to build broader manager trust on.
